Welcome to EduCorr. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and disclose your information, including personal information, in connection with your access to and use of the EduCorr application.

Important Notice: By using EduCorr, you consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use EduCorr, we may collect information that you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and other account credentials.
  • Educational Content: Text, documents, assignments, and other educational materials you submit for correction or assessment.
  • Communications: Information you provide when you contact us for support or feedback.
  • Profile Information: Optional information you choose to add to your profile, such as educational level, subjects of interest, or profile pictures.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use EduCorr, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access EduCorr, including hardware model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Information: Details about how you use our application, including access times, pages viewed, features used, and other system activity.
  • Log Information: Server logs that include your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and other diagnostic data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve EduCorr's services
  • Process and correct educational materials you submit
  • Personalize your experience and deliver content relevant to your educational needs
  • Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends to improve EduCorr
  • Protect against, identify, and prevent fraud and other illegal activities
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We will share your information when you direct us to do so.
  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, analytics, and customer support.

4. Data Security

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:
